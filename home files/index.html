About-Us

A non-political, student-oriented scientific club in Abdelhamid Mehri University (Constantine2)that represents Mozilla and works for its purpose to make the Web accessible for everyone no matter what background he has, in addition, this club works to make technologies more open to people by supporting open source projects. We have a highly collaborative environment where all members share their skills and knowledge through events, conferences and small projects that focus on society and students from other fields. Who is Mozilla Firefox?

Firefox, in full Mozilla Firefox, free open-source Web browser created by the American software company Mozilla Corporation. In 1998 the American Internet services company Netscape Communications Corp. decided to designate its Navigator browser as open-source for users, who began the development of Mozilla Firefox.
